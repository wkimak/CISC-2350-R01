What is the ClothBot 3D43?

The ClothBot is a 3D Clothing Printer. It is a wall-mounted appliance that is connected electronically to clothing companies across the globe. Top companies include Nike, American Eagle, Tommy Hilfiger, Coach, Michael Kors, Gap, Ralph lauren, Under Armour, and much more. The ClothBot's touch-screen display will allow you to see all of your options.

Product Features

  • Intuitive Interface
  • Custom Measurements
  • Reviews
  • Fast Printing

Why 3D Clothes Printer?

No need to face big crowds at the mall or walk aimlessly, trying to find your size. The ClothBot takes your measurements and shows your options on its intuitive interface. Each product has reviews attached so you aware of what you are purchasing. The ClothBot is also simple, just click "Purchase and Print" on selected items. Spend freely and see your purchase instanateously.
