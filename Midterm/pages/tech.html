3D Printing

3D printing is the process of making three dimensioanl solid objects from a digitial file. Your clothes are created using additive processes. This involves laying down successive layers of material until your clothing item is created. Clothbot combines efficiency with quality, printing beautiful apparel in no more then 30 minutes.

Measurements

The user can input their measurements to filter out unwanted clothing items.

Measurements include total height, shoulder circumference, chest circumference, waist circumference, high-hip circumferences, hip circumference, and inseam length.

These measurements are then put into an algorithm to retrieve the best options. ClothBot's system will save your measurements for future endeavors.

Display Screen

Clothbot's touch screen display is tablet-like and simple to use. Our goal of the design is to get you to your destination with fewer clicks. The system architecture is built by industry leaders to provide security and quick installation. Just plug your measurements and trust the process.
