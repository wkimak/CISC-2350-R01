Martin Scorsese

A Creative Influential

Martin Scorsese is an American director, producer, screenwriter, and film historian. He was born in 1942 and has had a career that spans more than 50 years.
He is the founder of The Film Foundation and the World Cinema Foundation . He has directed a variety of films, starring incredible actors.

My favorite films of his include:

  1. Goodfellas (1990)
  2. Gangs of New York (2002)
  3. The Aviator (2004)
  4. The Departed (2006)
  5. Shutter Island (2010)
  6. The Wolf of Wall Street (2013)
